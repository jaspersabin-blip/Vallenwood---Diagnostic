What the Revenue System Score measures

The Vallenwood Revenue System Score evaluates how well your go-to-market system creates and delivers value to your target buyers. It looks at five areas of the commercial engine — how you position in the market, how you articulate value, how you price and package, how you go to market, and how you measure what works.

Each area is scored out of 20. The total reflects overall system alignment. More importantly, it identifies which area is most likely acting as a constraint on the others — because in most businesses, one weak area limits what every other area can achieve.

What this report is not

This report is not a verdict. It is a lens. The patterns it surfaces are indicators worth exploring — not certainties about your business. A diagnostic based on a structured intake can reveal meaningful signals, but it cannot replace the full picture that comes from sitting in the room with your team. Use it as a starting point for the right conversations, not as a prescription to follow without question.
